**Meridian National Bank**

## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the standards, requirements, and procedures governing the origination of residential mortgage loans at Meridian National Bank ("the Bank"). It applies to all conventional, FHA, VA, and USDA loan products originated through retail and consumer direct channels.

## 2. Scope

- All residential first-lien mortgage loans (1-4 unit properties)
- Home equity lines of credit (HELOC) when originated alongside first-lien
- Refinance transactions (rate/term and cash-out)
- Construction-to-permanent loans

**Exclusions**: Commercial real estate, multifamily (5+ units), purchased loan portfolios.

## 4. Origination Channels

### 4.1 Retail Branch
Loan Officers (NMLS-registered) conduct face-to-face or phone originations. Each LO must maintain:
- Active NMLS registration
- Annual compliance training completion
- Production minimum of 4 loans/month (rolling 6-month average)

### 4.2 Consumer Direct
Digital application via the Bank's website or mobile app. Automated pre-qualification with manual underwriting review required for all approvals.

## 5. Application Process

### 5.1 Pre-Qualification
- Soft credit pull (no impact to borrower score)
- Verbal income and asset verification
- Preliminary product recommendation
- Pre-qualification letter valid for 90 days

### 5.2 Full Application (URLA/1003)
Loan Officer collects:
1. Completed Uniform Residential Loan Application
2. Government-issued photo ID
3. Most recent 2 years W-2s or 1099s
4. Most recent 30 days pay stubs
5. Most recent 2 months bank/asset statements (all pages)
6. Most recent 2 years federal tax returns (self-employed)
7. Authorization to pull credit

### 5.3 Disclosure Requirements
Within **3 business days** of application:
- Loan Estimate (LE) delivered
- Intent to Proceed obtained before ordering services
- Rate lock agreement (if applicable)

## 6. Credit and Risk Assessment

### 6.1 Credit Requirements
- Tri-merge credit report from approved vendor (currently Equifax/Experian/TransUnion via MeridianCredit)
- Representative credit score: median of three scores; if two borrowers, use lower median
- Credit alerts and fraud flags must be resolved before proceeding

### 6.2 Automated Underwriting
All loans run through:
- **DU (Desktop Underwriter)** for conventional/jumbo
- **LP (Loan Prospector)** as backup/comparison
- **TOTAL Scorecard** for FHA
- **WebLGY** for VA

AUS recommendation of Approve/Eligible or Accept required. Refer/Caution findings require manual underwrite with SVP approval.

### 6.3 Income Verification
| Employment Type | Verification Method |
|----------------|-------------------|
| W-2 Salaried | VOE + 2yr W-2 + 30-day paystubs |
| Hourly/Variable | VOE + 2yr W-2 + 30-day paystubs + 24-month avg |
| Self-Employed | 2yr tax returns + YTD P&L (CPA prepared) + business license |
| Retired | Award letters + 2yr tax returns + asset statements |
| Rental Income | Lease agreements + 2yr Schedule E + 2yr tax returns |

### 6.4 Asset Verification
- 2 months bank statements (all pages, no whiteouts)
- Large deposits (>50% of monthly income) require sourcing documentation
- Gift funds require gift letter + donor bank statement + transfer evidence
- Retirement/investment accounts: most recent quarterly statement

## 7. Property and Collateral

### 7.1 Appraisal
- Full interior/exterior appraisal required for all purchase transactions
- Appraisal waiver acceptable only when offered by AUS for refinance with LTV < 80%
- AMC rotation required; no direct LO-appraiser contact
- Appraisal must be ordered through Encompass AMC portal (currently ValuTrac)

### 7.2 Title and Insurance
- Clear title required with owner's and lender's title insurance
- Flood zone determination required; flood insurance if in SFHA
- Hazard insurance with replacement cost coverage minimum

## 8. Underwriting Decision Authority

| Decision | Authority Level |
|----------|----------------|
| AUS Approve/Eligible, no conditions | Underwriter I |
| AUS Approve with conditions | Underwriter II |
| Manual underwrite (non-complex) | Senior Underwriter |
| Manual underwrite (complex/exception) | Underwriting Manager |
| Exception to policy (DTI, LTV) | SVP Lending + Chief Credit Officer |
| Exception to policy (FICO) | Chief Credit Officer |

All exceptions require documented compensating factors and are logged in the Exception Tracking System.

## 9. Closing and Funding

### 9.1 Pre-Closing
- Final Closing Disclosure (CD) delivered minimum 3 business days before closing
- Changed circumstance documentation for any LE-to-CD tolerance violations
- Clear-to-close issued by underwriter after all conditions satisfied

### 9.2 Closing
- Wet-sign or authorized e-close (DocuSign via Encompass)
- Notary must verify borrower identity
- Funding authorization within 24 hours of document receipt

### 9.3 Post-Close QC
- 10% random sample of funded loans reviewed within 90 days
- 100% of early payment defaults (EPD) reviewed
- 100% of loans with exceptions reviewed
- Findings reported to Loan Quality Committee quarterly

## 10. Compliance Requirements

### 10.1 Regulatory Framework
- TILA/RESPA Integrated Disclosures (TRID)
- Equal Credit Opportunity Act (ECOA)
- Fair Housing Act
- Home Mortgage Disclosure Act (HMDA)
- Bank Secrecy Act (BSA/AML)
- OFAC screening requirements
- State-specific lending regulations

### 10.2 Fair Lending
- Prohibited basis factors never considered in lending decisions
- Comparative file analysis conducted quarterly
- HMDA LAR data reviewed for disparities monthly
- Second review program for denied applications

### 10.3 HMDA Reporting
All required data fields captured at application, action, and denial. LAR submitted annually per CFPB requirements.

## 11. Servicing Transfer

Loans may be retained for servicing or transferred to approved sub-servicers:
- **Retained**: Conventional conforming, jumbo portfolio
- **Transferred**: FHA/VA/USDA (sub-serviced by MeridianServ LLC)
- Borrower notification of servicing transfer per RESPA requirements

## 12. Document Retention

| Document Type | Retention Period |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Loan file (complete) | Life of loan + 7 years |
| Denied applications | 25 months from action date |
| HMDA data | 3 years from submission |
| Compliance training records | 5 years |
