Architecture Decision: Extension, Not Separate Bot

Decision intelligence is implemented as a Decision Knowledge Module within KM4ProcessBot, not a separate KM4DecisionBot. Decisions are discovered in context of processes (at gateways), the 9 Knowledge Forms architecture already integrates Rules as Form 5, and evidence sources feed both process and decision knowledge. A separate bot would create artificial graph boundaries.
