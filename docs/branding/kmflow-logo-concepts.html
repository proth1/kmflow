KMFlow Logo Concepts

Ten distinct logo directions for the AI-powered Process Intelligence platform. KMFlow ingests diverse client evidence, builds semantic knowledge graphs, and generates confidence-scored process views for consulting engagements.

Concept 01
Flowing Nodes
Connected process nodes with flowing curved paths. Neural networks meet workflow orchestration. Deep blue to teal gradient conveys depth and intelligence.
Concept 02
Abstract Wave Ribbons
Overlapping curved ribbons forming a subtle "K" shape. Blue to violet palette conveys innovation and premium technology. Dynamic yet elegant.
Concept 03
Gear + Flow
Partial stylized gear (automation engine) with flowing lines emerging outward. Indigo and amber create strong contrast. Engineering meets intelligence.
Concept 04
Diamond Paths
BPMN gateway diamond with three graceful decision paths. Emerald to teal palette directly references process intelligence and decision flow analysis.
Concept 05
Neural Process
Neural network meets directed process flows in a circular container. Dark slate with cyan accents. Intellectual, sophisticated AI aesthetic.
Concept 06
Knowledge Graph
Interconnected semantic graph nodes radiating from a central hub. Red-orange palette signals energy and transformation. Represents the knowledge graph core of KMFlow.
Concept 07
Stacked Layers
Three ascending layers (evidence, process, insight) with a forward-pointing flow arrow. Sky blue palette. Represents the transformation pipeline from data to knowledge.
Concept 08
Monogram Shield
Bold "KM" monogram inside a rounded square container. Purple gradient on dark background. Premium app-icon aesthetic - works as favicon and mobile icon out of the box.
Concept 09
Convergence Arrows
Three diverse evidence streams converging into a single insight arrow. Teal, dark, and blue inputs unify through a central node. Captures the evidence-first methodology.
Concept 10
Infinity Process
Figure-8/infinity loop symbolizing continuous process improvement cycles. Rose to purple to blue gradient. Conveys perpetual knowledge flow and iterative refinement.
